# E-commerce Sales Analysis Report

## Overview
This project analyzes e-commerce sales data to understand product performance and sales trends.

## Key Analysis
- Total revenue calculated from sales data
- Best-selling product identified using aggregation
- Monthly sales trend analyzed

## Visual Insights
- Bar chart shows which products contribute most to revenue
- Line chart shows sales trend over months

## Conclusion
The analysis helps understand customer demand and seasonal sales patterns, supporting better business decisions.
